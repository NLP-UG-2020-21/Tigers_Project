Hi! We are Tigers
A group of students from Poland focused on the development of NLP tools. With this RawrTextAnalyser we hope to provide a tool for simple text analysis. The project is still in development with new tools being added to it over time. Our aim is to gather the most frequently used tools in one place for your convinience.
To read about the currently available tools go to the About the Tool section of our website.
